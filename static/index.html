Music Tier List Maker

MTLM is an advanced interactable tier list maker. It was designed for making song tier lists as its really hard show people your rankings when you just see album covers. MTLM provides the solution! MTLM lets you add a title, description, image, and link to each item on your tier list. By simply hoving over the image, you can see all the information your viewers need to know.
